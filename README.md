# EcoTrack-Carbon-Footprint
EcoTrack is a web-based carbon footprint calculator that helps users estimate their daily CO₂ emissions from travel and electricity usage while promoting eco-friendly habits.
#  EcoTrack: Carbon Footprint Calculator

##  Elevator Pitch
Track your daily carbon footprint and discover simple ways to reduce your environmental impact.

---

## Overview
EcoTrack is a simple web-based application that helps users calculate their daily carbon footprint based on everyday activities like transportation and electricity usage.

This project is designed to raise awareness about environmental impact and encourage users to make eco-friendly lifestyle choices.

---

## Features
- Calculate daily CO₂ emissions
- Travel-based emission calculation
- Electricity usage tracking
- Instant feedback (Low / High impact)
- Simple and beginner-friendly interface

---

##  Built With
- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript  

---

##  How It Works
Users input:
- Distance traveled (in kilometers)
- Electricity usage (in kWh)

The application calculates:
- Carbon emissions from travel
- Carbon emissions from electricity
- Total daily carbon footprint

---

##  Environmental Impact
EcoTrack helps users:
- Understand their contribution to carbon emissions  
- Make informed decisions about energy and travel  
- Adopt sustainable habits to reduce environmental damage 


##  Installation & Usage
1. Download or clone the repository
2. Open `index.html` in your browser
3. Enter your data and calculate your carbon footprint
